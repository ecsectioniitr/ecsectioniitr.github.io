Lecture Series on ANN, Microcontroller and Spring of Learning

A short lecture series was conducted by the students of third year on neural networks. The series covered basics of neural networks and machine learning, and to some extent decision trees.
A short introductory lecture on microcontrollers specifically arduino, was conducted for freshmen with the collaboration from ASME IIT Roorkee chapter. More than 50 students attended the lecture.
With the collaboration from ASME, ArIES is conducting a workshop on line following robot where freshmen will be given their own kits in a team of 4 or 5. This is to provide them practical hands-on experience in developing a prototype.
All throughout this semester, there are going to be many lectures and workshops for the freshmen followed by small project assignments. This is to provide them with better insights in the world of electronics and AI.
